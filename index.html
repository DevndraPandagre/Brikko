Frequently Asked Questions

What is Brikko?
Brikko is a trusted B2B marketplace that connects users with verified contractors, suppliers, manufacturers, and consultants across construction, engineering, and industrial sectors.
How are businesses verified on Brikko?
Businesses are verified based on company registration, experience, portfolio, customer feedback, and industry reputation to ensure trust and reliability.
Why should I use Brikko instead of searching manually?
Brikko saves time by providing verified listings, transparent profiles, easy comparison, and direct access to contractors, suppliers, manufacturers, and consultants in one platform.
Can I find service providers near me?
Yes. Brikko helps you find local contractors, suppliers, manufacturers, and consultants based on your location and project requirements.
How does Brikko work?
Users search and connect with verified businesses, compare services, review profiles, and directly contact contractors, suppliers, manufacturers, or consultants for their projects.
Can I compare multiple quotations or businesses?
Yes. You can request and compare multiple quotations and evaluate businesses based on pricing, experience, reviews, and service quality.
What types of businesses are listed on Brikko?
Brikko features contractors, suppliers, manufacturers, and consultants across construction, infrastructure, engineering, and industrial sectors.
Can I view previous projects and reviews?
Yes. Verified businesses showcase portfolios, completed projects, certifications, and customer reviews to help you make informed decisions.

Frequently Asked Question

You May Ask

How are builders ranked on the platform?
Builders are ranked based on:
  • Customer ratings
  • Project completion history
  • Response time
  • Experience
  • Verification status
  • Service quality
How do I know if a quote is reasonable?
We recommend comparing at least three quotes and reviewing what is included, such as materials, labor, warranties, and timelines.
Why use this platform instead of searching manually?
  • Verified builders
  • Multiple quotes
  • Easy comparison
  • Faster hiring process
  • Free homeowner accession
Can I find builders near me?
Yes. Our platform helps you discover trusted builders and renovation experts in your local area.
How does this platform work?
Our platform connects homeowners with verified builders, contractors, architects, and renovation experts. Simply submit your project requirements and receive quotes from suitable professionals.
Are the builders on your platform verified?
Yes. We verify business registrations, experience, portfolios, and customer feedback before listing builders.
Can I get quotes from multiple builders?
Yes. You can receive and compare quotes from multiple builders to find the best option for your budget and requirements.
Can I view previous projects completed by builders?
Yes. Most builders showcase project photos, customer reviews, and portfolios on their profiles.
